About us

Supportive Community

From the moment you join our virtual airline, you'll experience the warmth, respect, and hospitality that define Sri Lanka. Our community reflects the values of the nation's flag carrier while welcoming every pilot with the spirit of Ayubowan. Whether you're taking your very first virtual airline flight or are an experienced pilot, you'll find a respectful and welcoming community ready to support, encourage, and fly alongside you.

Realistic Airline Operations

Our operations are built around carefully designed schedules, authentic route planning, realistic fleet assignments and operational procedures, providing an immersive virtual airline experience from departure to arrival. Explore an extensive network spanning regional services across the Indian Ocean, the Middle East, and South Asia, together with long-haul routes linking Sri Lanka to major destinations around the world.

Integrated Flight Tracking and Management System

Track your progress through vAMSYS, complete flights, build your career, and fly within an organised environment that values consistency and realism. Looking ahead, we are proud to be developing our own Electronic Flight Bag (EFB) and ACARS platform, designed exclusively for SriLankan Airlines Virtual. We look forward to introducing an even more seamless and immersive operational experience in the near future.
